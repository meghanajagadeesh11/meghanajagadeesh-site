Thinking

What I keep coming back to.

AI products are easy to demo and hard to make useful. The gap is usually not model quality alone. It is workflow, trust, distribution and timing.

I am interested in builders who understand the problem before they fall in love with the tool. Especially non-technical operators who have lived close enough to a market to know what is broken.

I speak about this at events like QE Conclave and Cypher by AIM, and write more often on LinkedIn.
